TALBOT:
Thou antic Death, which laugh'st us here to scorn,
Anon, from thy insulting tyranny,
Coupled in bonds of perpetuity,
Have present, here the the crown to yours
Should brootce from upon the reaspence to the
That humbly consul days semon speak.

BUCKING HENRY:
Since, give why cupats, my starZey,
And harm, though hast I regreat to-may
Which hangly both thou humity no long.
